The Fur Trade: Calgary Scavenger Hunt

Location: Heritage Park

This is an old Hudson’s Bay trading fort. This was built by Heritage Park. In 1956 in efforts to re-create the original trading post in 1865. This is super realistic and has some real artifacts from the original.
